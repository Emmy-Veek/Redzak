Our Vision

Redzak’s mission is to become the one place you’ll ever have to go for every stage of the home-buying process. It’s a revolutionary idea, one that relentlessly looks at the experience through the eyes of those that matter most: people who want a home.

How Redzak Works

Most real estate sites leverage a database of home listings to attract interested buyers. How the sites turn a profit is by selling these “leads” to real estate agents. Redzak is different. Find out how real estate professionals become part of the Redzak ecosystem. In exchange, they receive a comprehensive suite of tools to help them serve the homebuyer as only Redzak can. By foregoing the practice of monetizing leads, Redzak gains the flexibility to innovate in the real estate space.
